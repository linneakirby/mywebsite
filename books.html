Anosmia Perfumery

Spring 2016


Anosmia Perfumery explores the dichotomous relationship between smell and anosmia (the inability to smell) and their interaction with society.

Clamshell box enclosing anosmic perfume samples and their descriptions. Each smell description was obtained from a different source, using the prompt "What is your favorite smell and how would you describe it?"



History of Hysteria: A Timeline in Comic Form

Spring 2016


History of Hysteria drolly criticizes female hysteria through the illustrated presentation of pivotal moments in the "illness"'s history beginning with Hippocrates's "wandering womb" and ending with Alabama's 2009 upholding of sex toys for medical use only.

Accordian fold with hand-carved, stamped, and embossed uterus background and hand-drawn cartoons. Disguised to look like a reference manual on female hysteria and accompanied by an embossed vibrator.



Distill.java and Exception

Spring 2016

This pair of books present the artist's experience and struggles as an introductory computer science student. Distill.java and Exception are intended to be read side-by-side, as Exception provides explanations, both technical and metaphorical, to the output presented on each page in Distill.java.

Invented binding, Java code for a text-distillation program, Oracle descriptions of various exceptions, metaphorical text explaining the exceptions.



Story Book

Fall 2012


Story Book explores the author's childhood summers in Norway through collage and free-form poetry.

Coptic binding with collage and rubber stamping. Covered with flowered origami paper.



The King's Breakfast

Fall 2012


The King's Breakfast presents a typographical representation of A.A. Milne's poem of the same title.

Coptic binding with hand-drawn typography. Covered with flowered origami paper.



Immigration

Fall 2012

Immigration proffers Meeko Israel's poem of the same name across a cityscape of New York City.

Accordion-fold with hand-drawn cityscape and typed vellum pages.
