append

Dance and Partner Acrobatics - Oberlin - 2016

append combines elements of dance, circus, sound, and technology to create an immersive experience that explores the interconnectivity between people and technology. append is comprised of five distinct sections: interaction, connection, distraction, construction, and consumption.

Choreographed by Molly Barger, sound by Christiana Rose.
