Linnea Kirby is a recent graduate of Oberlin College, where she received a B.A. in Psychology with a concentration in Cognitive Sciences and a minor in Computer Science. She would have majored in Computer Science, but she discovered her love of coding and problem-solving late in her Oberlin career, and so she is learning as much as she can now. While at Oberlin, she was also an administrator and active member of OCircus!, Oberlin's student-run circus.

Her home base is the Boston area, but she was born in Sweden and has lived in Norway and Puerto Rico as well. She spent Fall semester of her Junior year in Brussels, Belgium at the Université Libre de Bruxelles attempting to improve her French, with mixed success (though she did learn some Dutch).

Linnea enjoys pursuing artistic endeavors, the circus arts, and coding, among other things.
