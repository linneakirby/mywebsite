Linnea Kirby is a 4th year at Oberlin College, where she is majoring in Psychology and minoring in Computer Science. She is also an administrator and active member of OCircus!, Oberlin's student-run circus.

Her home base is the Boston area, but she was born in Sweden and has lived in Norway and Puerto Rico as well. She spent Fall semester of her Junior year in Brussels, Belgium at the Université Libre de Bruxelles attempting to improve her French, with mixed success (though she did learn some Dutch).

Linnea enjoys pursuing artistic endeavors, the circus arts, and coding, among other things. She is still trying to figure out what to do with her life.
